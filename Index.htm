Airtable Lightnotes

What is Lightnotes?

How to utilize Airtable Lightnotes

 

The Idea

 

 

I use Airtable to organize everything, and it really does improves my productivity and performance since I am able to list all my goals and stay focused by tracking my steps everyday. However I still use the "Notes" application on my computer, ipad and even a physical piece of paper to instantly note ideas, reminders and bulletpoints of my daily goals. Seeing an outline of my day or week helps me stay on track, focused and manage my time better.

Lightnotes was derived from notepad and highlights. This feature is a mixture of both. You can highlight important records in your bases and also note down your ideas before forming them into real records.

 

 

How does it work?

 

 

Let's say you work in multiple projects and need something like a dashboard to see highlights from different projects at the same time while you are working or want to note down an idea that just popped in your mind.

Select a line from a base and link it to your board. Rename or delete the link if you want.

Airtable Lightnotes is basically a central and lightweight dashboard for daily/weekly tasks and reminders.

With this feature, other channels are eliminated and airtable is the only source of information on all of your devices.
